About

A multidisciplinary communicator across sustainability, research, healthcare, and digital media.

Julie is a research and strategic communications professional with a multidisciplinary background in journalism, sustainability and supply chain studies, research support, community healthcare, and digital communication.

Her career began in China as a senior television journalist and program producer, where she developed strong skills in storytelling, interviewing, public communication, and managing complex media projects. In Canada, she has expanded her work into research communication, community health interpretation, sustainability-oriented learning, and public-facing digital content.

She is currently strengthening her technical skill set through self-directed learning in Python, HTML/CSS, GitHub, and AI-assisted workflows, with a long-term interest in human-centered technology for sustainability, healthcare, and public communication.
